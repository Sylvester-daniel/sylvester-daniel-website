How a project actually runs

  1. 01

    Discover

    A short call or email thread about your business, your customers, and what "done" looks like — plus a fixed quote before anything starts.

  2. 02

    Design

    You'll see real layouts and drafts early, not a wall of text — so changes happen before build, when they're cheap.

  3. 03

    Build & edit

    Site build, store setup, video cuts or design files come together, with progress check-ins so nothing lands as a surprise.

  4. 04

    Launch & grow

    Go live, get trained on the basics, and keep the option of ongoing support, new content, and future updates.
