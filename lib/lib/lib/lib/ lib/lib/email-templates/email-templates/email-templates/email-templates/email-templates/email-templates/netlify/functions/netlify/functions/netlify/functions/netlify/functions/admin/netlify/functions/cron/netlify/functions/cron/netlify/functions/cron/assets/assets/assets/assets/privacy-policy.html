Who I am

This website and its mailing list are operated by Sylvester Daniel, an independent web design, development, video editing and graphic design practice based in Edinburgh, United Kingdom. For the purposes of UK data protection law, I am the data controller for the personal data described in this policy.

What I collect

I only collect what's needed to run the mailing list and reply to enquiries.

How I use it

  • Sending a welcome email when you subscribe, or a welcome-back email if you rejoin
  • Sending a birthday email once a year
  • Sending a "happy new month" email on the first of the month
  • Sending holiday greetings (Christmas, New Year, Easter, and similar)
  • Sending occasional promotional offers or announcements of new services
  • Sending a thank-you email after certain interactions, such as a first enquiry
  • Sending a goodbye confirmation if you unsubscribe
  • Replying to project enquiries sent by email

I do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to anyone.

Legal basis for processing

Under UK GDPR, I rely on:

  • Consent — for sending marketing and automated emails (welcome, birthday, holiday, promotional, and announcement emails). You give this consent by ticking the checkbox on the subscribe form, and can withdraw it at any time.
  • Legitimate interests — for replying to enquiries you send me directly, and for basic spam and abuse prevention (such as rate limiting and honeypot checks).

Sharing & processors

I use the following third-party services to run the mailing list and website. Each acts as a data processor on my behalf and only handles your data to provide their service:

  • Brevo (email marketing & automation platform) — stores your contact details and email engagement history to send and track the emails listed above.
  • Netlify (website hosting) — hosts this website and the backend functions that process form submissions.
  • Google reCAPTCHA — used on the subscribe form to reduce spam and automated abuse. Google's own privacy policy applies to its use.

None of these providers are permitted to use your data for their own marketing purposes.

How long I keep it

Subscriber data is kept for as long as you remain subscribed. If you unsubscribe, your contact record is suppressed from future sends and retained only as required to honour that suppression and meet legal obligations, then deleted. Enquiry emails are kept for as long as reasonably needed to handle the enquiry and any follow-up, typically no longer than 24 months.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data I hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time (unsubscribe link in every email, or email me directly)
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO)

To exercise any of these rights, email sylvester76daniel@gmail.com — I'll respond within one month.

Cookies & reCAPTCHA

This site does not use tracking or advertising cookies. The subscribe form uses Google reCAPTCHA to prevent automated spam submissions, which may set its own cookies and process limited technical data (such as your IP address) under Google's privacy policy.

Children

This mailing list and website are intended for business owners aged 18 and over. I do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.

Security

Form submissions are protected with honeypot fields, timing checks, and reCAPTCHA to reduce spam and abuse. Data in transit is encrypted (HTTPS), and access to the mailing list platform is protected by account credentials and API keys stored as environment variables, never in the website's source code.

Changes to this policy

I may update this policy occasionally to reflect changes in how the site or mailing list works. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the latest version.
